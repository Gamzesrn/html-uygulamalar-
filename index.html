The official website of World Rowing, the international rowing federation. News, live coverage, biographies, calendar, videos, photos

There are many ways to become part of the rowing community. As a rower, a coach, an official or a supporter. As a rower the opportunities include flat water, coastal, indoor, para as well as age group rowing including masters, university, Under 23 and Under 19. For those looking to develop rowing further, World Rowing has compiled a toolkit in order to (further) develop rowing in your country.
